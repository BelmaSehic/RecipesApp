A classic Italian pasta dish with rich meat sauce. A delicious and hearty meal perfect for dinner!

Preparation time: 30 min

Added by: John Doe

Added on: March 15, 2025

Category: Italian

Instructions:

1. Heat a pan and cook the ground beef until browned.
2. Add chopped onions, garlic, and cook until soft.
3. Stir in tomato sauce, salt, pepper, and cook for 20 minutes.
4. Serve over cooked spaghetti and garnish with Parmesan cheese.
